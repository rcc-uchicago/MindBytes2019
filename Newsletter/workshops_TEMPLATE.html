Introduction to the Digital Humanities
Jeffrey Tharsen, Scientific Programming Consultant, Research Computing Center
Tuesday, July 26, 2016, 2 p.m. – 3:30 p.m. | Kathleen A. Zar Room, John Crerar Library
Digital Humanities is a growing, interdisciplinary endeavor aimed at uniting technology and humanistic inquiry. This workshop is a basic theoretical and methodological introduction to the use and development of digital toolkits, digital texts and digital media.
Level:This is an INTRODUCTORY level course, for people with little or no experience in the topic.
 
Prerequisites:Attendees are encouraged to bring a laptop to participate in the hands-on session
